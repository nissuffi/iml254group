ABOUT US 

Welcome to AWAY Novel Preloved!

Our goal at AWAY Novel Preloved is to spark readers' interests with a carefully selected range of used books. We want to give our valuable novels a "new home" with different publishers, newly released books, and genres because we believe in the power of stories. 

Our Values

Community

We unite readers of novels to identify the publishers and genres they are interested in.

Sustainability

We support environmentally friendly reading by providing reasonably priced novels to new homes.

Quality

Every book is hand-selected with care to guarantee that it lives up to the user's high expectations.

Our Collection

Explore our wide range of second-hand books, which includes both classics and modern bestsellers. Every book has an origin and a tale that is just waiting for you to rediscover.  

Why Choose AWAY?

Novel enthusiasts pick us because of our commitment, longevity, and excellence. Choosing second-hand books not only benefits the environment but also offers fantastic reads at reasonable costs.  

Get in Touch

It would be a pleasure to get in touch with other book lovers! Feel free to contact us using the platform we've provided, whether you're looking for a specific title or have inquiries about our collection!

Thank you for choosing AWAY Novel Preloved. Happy reading!
